Title: Better selling through a web-site

<b>Better selling through a web-site</b>. <a href="http://www.marketingprofs.com/3/dsouza24.asp">This article</a> talks about how to better sell via web-site. It's a bit long-winded but the message is simple: provide as much information as possible e.g. if you sell software, put your manuals, detailed descriptions, screenshots, testimonials etc. on-line. The more the better.

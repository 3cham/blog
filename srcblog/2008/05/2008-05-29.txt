Title: SumatraPDF 0.8.1

Shipping feels good, I just shipped 0.8.1 version of SumatraPDF hence I feel good. Changes in new version:
<ul>
	<li>automatic reloading of changed PDFs (contributed by William Blum)</li>
	<li>tex integration (contributed by William Blum)</li>
	<li>updated icon for case-sensitivity selection in find (contributed by Sonke Tesch)</li>
	<li>language change is now a separate dialog instead of a menu</li>
	<li>remember more settings (like default view)</li>
	<li>automatic checks for new versions</li>
	<li>add command-line option -lang $lang</li>
	<li>add command-line option -print-dialog (contributed by Peter Astrand)</li>
	<li>ESC or single mouse click hides selection</li>
	<li>fix showing boxes in table of contents tree</li>
	<li>translation updates (contributed by <a href="http://blog.kowalczyk.info/software/sumatrapdf/translators.html">many people</a>)</li>
</ul>
Go <a href="http://blog.kowalczyk.info/software/sumatrapdf/index.html">get it</a>.

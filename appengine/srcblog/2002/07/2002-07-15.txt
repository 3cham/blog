Title: Laws of marketing #19 (failure)

<a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0887306667">The
book</a> says: failure is to be expected and accepted. Drop things that don't work 
instead of trying to fix them. Don't punish for failures (if you do people will 
stop taking risks).

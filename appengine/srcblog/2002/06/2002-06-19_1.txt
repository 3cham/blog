Title: Blogging is a marathon

Blogging is a seductive idea, so many people try it. It's also a lot 
of work, so many people stop after a week or two. I've seen that 
already a few times. So if you're starting a blog, remember: blogging 
is a marathong, not a sprint. Plan for that.

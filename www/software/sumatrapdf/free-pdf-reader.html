Sumatra PDF

A PDF reader for Windows


What is Sumatra PDF?

Sumatra PDF is a free PDF, XPS, DjVu, CHM, CBZ and CBR reader for Windows.

Sumatra PDF is small, portable and starts up very fast.

Simplicity of the user interface has a high priority.

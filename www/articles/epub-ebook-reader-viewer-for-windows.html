List of ePub format readers and viewers for Windows

ePub readers and viewers

Sumatra PDF

Sumatra PDF supports viewing mobi ebooks since version 2.1. Sumatra is free, open-source and supports many other formats, like PDF, mobi, XPS, DjVu, CBR, CBZ.

FBReader

FBReader is a cross-platform, open-source eBook reader that can also read ePub files.

Mobipocket Reader

Mobipocket Reader supports ePub format (among others).

Cool Reader

Cool Reader supports ePub and a few other formats.

Details of ePub format

ePUb ePub is an open format defined by the Open eBook Forum of the International Digital Publishing Forum.

ePub format is a based on XHTML, XML and CSS.

Mobi files usually have .mobi extension. Amazon's eBooks have .azw extension. Because the format was originally designed for Palm Pilot PDAs, they can also have .prc or .pdb extension.
